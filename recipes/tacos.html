Chicken Tacos

Description

These chicken tacos are perfect for those nights when you don't have a lot of time for dinner preparations.

Ingredients

  • 1lb chicken brest
  • Olive oil
  • Lime juice
  • Cheddar cheese
  • Tortillas
  • Lettuce
  • Tomatoes

Steps

  1. Cook chicken in a large skillet over medium heat
  2. Meanwhile, warm the tortillas in the oven or microwave until soft.
  3. When chicken is fully cooked, transfer to serving bowl.
  4. Place chopped tomatoes, lettuce, cheese, salsa, and sour cream in serving dishes.
  5. Each person can create their own wrap, using their preferred ingredients.
